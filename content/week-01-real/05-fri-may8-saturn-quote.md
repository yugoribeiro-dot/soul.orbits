Saturn is the most misunderstood planet in modern astrology.

The internet calls it the "great malefic" — the planet of restriction, hardship, things being taken away. That reading came from medieval astrology, when surviving a Saturn return often meant just surviving. We don't live in that world anymore.

Modern Saturn, read evolutionarily, is the editor. The part of your chart that asks: "what's load-bearing here, and what's just decoration?" It removes the decoration. Sometimes painfully. Always for a reason.

The thing that "Saturn took away" almost always turns out to be the thing that wouldn't have held weight long-term anyway. Saturn just notices it sooner than you do.

With Saturn in Aries now (Feb 2026 - April 2028), the audit is on identity. What you've called "yourself" that isn't actually yours.
