## Template C — Reflection / Quote

**Background:** `#0B0A1F` with a faint, off-center starfield (very subtle — barely visible). Optional: a single tiny gold dot at golden-ratio position to anchor the eye.

**Layout:**

```
│                                         │

```

**Caption template:**
```
[Optional: 1–3 line expansion of the quote, OR the bare quote alone]

—

#evolutionaryastrology #astrology #saturn
```

**Composition notes:**
- The point is the breath around the words. Resist filling the canvas.
- No glyph, no logo wordmark in the corner — the attribution line is enough
- Quote must fit on 2 lines max — if it doesn't, it's not a quote post

---

## Production notes for all templates
- Export at 1080×1350 (4:5) when possible — uses more feed real estate; still works in grid
- Always check legibility on a phone at arm's length before shipping
- For carousels: design slide 1 last, after the rest is written — it has to earn the swipe
