# soul.orbits — Content Pillars

Five pillars. Each post must belong to exactly one. If a post doesn't fit a pillar, it doesn't ship.

## 1. Daily Transit (40% — 3 posts/week)
The flagship. Today's most significant transit, framed as a reflective prompt.

- **Format:** Single image, dark background, Moon/planet glyph, 2–4 line caption on the image
- **Hook formula:** `[Planet] [moves into / aspects / stations] [sign/planet]. [One-line invitation to notice].`
- **Caption length:** 60–120 words on the post body
- **Example title:** *"Moon enters Scorpio. The buried wants air."*

## 2. Chart Literacy (20% — 1–2 posts/week)
Educational carousels that teach a single natal chart concept without jargon.

- **Format:** 6–8 slide carousel, one idea per slide
- **Concepts to rotate:** Big 3, the houses, aspects, the nodes, retrograde planets in natal, chart ruler, stelliums, MC/IC
- **Hook formula:** `[Misconception]. [Real meaning, in plain language].`
- **Example title:** *"Your Rising sign isn't your personality. It's your threshold."*

## 3. Reflection / Quote (20% — 1–2 posts/week)
The shareable. A single line of cosmic-philosophical writing on a quiet background.

- **Format:** Single image, generous space, one sentence, attributed to *— soul.orbits*
- **Source material:** Original lines, lightly inspired by Jung, Liz Greene, Steven Forrest, Caroline Casey, Rilke
- **Caption length:** 0–20 words on post body — let the image speak
- **Example:** *"Saturn doesn't punish. It edits."*

## 4. Sky This Week (10% — 1 post/week, Sundays)
Weekly forecast carousel — what to watch in the sky, in plain language.

- **Format:** 5–7 slide carousel: cover → 3–5 transits of the week → closing reflection
- **Cover formula:** `The week of [date]: [one-phrase theme]`
- **Tone:** more practical, less mystical — this is the "user manual" pillar
- **Example title:** *"The week of Nov 17: three doors open."*

## 5. Reel — Cosmic Slow Loop (10% — 1 reel/week, ideally Wed)
A 20–40 second slow video. Ambient music, celestial visuals, one quiet voiceover line or text overlay.

- **Format:** Vertical 9:16, no fast cuts, no trending audio
- **Visuals:** Moon phases, slow zoom on starfield, eclipse footage, abstract orbit animations
- **Audio:** Ambient pad (Brian Eno-style) — never trending sounds
- **Text overlay:** ONE sentence, fades in slowly, holds for 3+ seconds
- **Note:** Counter-programs the Reels meta on purpose — this is brand, not virality

## Weekly mix template

| Day | Pillar | Format |
|-----|--------|--------|
| Mon | Daily Transit | Single image |
| Tue | Chart Literacy | Carousel |
| Wed | Reel — Cosmic Slow Loop | Reel |
| Thu | Daily Transit | Single image |
| Fri | Reflection / Quote | Single image |
| Sat | Daily Transit | Single image |
| Sun | Sky This Week | Carousel |

**Why this mix:**
- 3 daily transits seed the "show up consistently" muscle
- Carousels (Tue + Sun) drive saves — the metric that compounds
- Reel midweek breaks up the static feed and reaches non-followers
- Quote on Friday is the most shareable day for reflective content
- No post on the same pillar two days in a row

## What we explicitly DON'T post
- Sun sign horoscopes ("Aries today: …")
- Compatibility content ("which sign is your soulmate")
- Crystal/tarot/manifestation tips
- Numerology, angel numbers
- Personal stories or face-to-camera reels
- Trending audio reels
- Engagement-bait questions ("what's your sign? 👇")
